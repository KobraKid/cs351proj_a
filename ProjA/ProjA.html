Menus
  • Press / to toggle this menu

Scene Navigation
  • Press WASD
          or ↑↓←→ to pan view
  • Press + and - to zoom in / out
  • Click and drag with the mouse to rotate view
  • Press P to globally pause / unpause animation
  • Press R to reset all user related manipulations
Object Manipulation
  • Press 12 ... 90 to change wind strength on cattails
  • Move the mouse to have dragonflies follow the mouse cursor
